We do

The objective of Alpha Degree Institute (AÎ¿ DEGREE) involves research and development of contemporary methods and tools of digital applications, design, marketing and branding in order to form a direct link between culture and tourism, to utilize domestic cultural reserves, and to promote elements and sites of culture and cultural heritage, housing complexes, as well as archaeological sites and monuments as experiential tourist destinations.


The Institute undertakes and executes projects from the stages of conception, planning, preparation of architectural, museological, museographic as well as technical specification designs, to the execution, construction, management and conservation of cultural projects, monuments and potential tourist sites. It also organizes lectures, presentations, conferences, publishes scientific studies and participates in European, national or international research programmes.


In addition, the Institute implements cultural awareness-raising actions on the protection and promotion of the cultural environment, and undertakes the digitization of material and intangible cultural content and cultural events.


Lastly, Alpha Degree Institute provides highly specialized scientific and counselling services in issues of cultural diplomacy as a means to strengthen transnational relations, intercultural dialogue and cooperation, the protection of human rights and the mitigation of racial, religious or other conflict.
